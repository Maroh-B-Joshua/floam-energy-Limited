Company Profile

Floam Energy Limited is an indigenous privately-traded oil and gas service company domiciled in warri, Delta State focused on procurement, sales & supply, construction and fabrication of oil fields In Nigeria.The management team of the company has significant experience in developing oil fields In the far regions of Nigeria and is led by an independent board of directors that is focused on safety and cost effectively developing and exploiting the Nigeria oil fields.

Services

Floam energy Limited is well known for its inexcusable procurement services.Thus, we Specialize in Welding and Fabrication of both oil fields and pipelines. Apart from pipeline construction, we ably indulge in maintenance also. To learn more about our services visit our procurement & logistics service page for more info.

Sales

One of our primary interest is procurement , sales & supply of line pipes , welding and fabrication materials , mechanical fittings and valves such as Flanges , Elbows , Tees , strainers , ball valves , globe valves , check valves etc. We also specialized in welding and fabrication , leasing and hiring of equipments such as Cranes , excavators , swap buggy , bulldozers etc .

Leasing & Hiring

Floam Energy Limited has compatible working construction vehicles. Hence, we lease out the finest Amphibious vehicles such as crain, excavators, swamp buggy, bull dozers, Trucks, Graders and Rollers etc. You can view photos of our work force in our Image gallery page.
