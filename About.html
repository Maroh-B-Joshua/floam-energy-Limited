About Us

Overview

Floam Energy Limited is an indigenous Nigeria oil and gas service company wholly owned, managed and operating exclusively by Nigerians with zero percent foreign stake, it was incorparated with the Corporate affairs commision in 2014 with registration number Rc1232115. Floam Energy Limited has its core business ; SALES, SUPPLY AND PROCUREMENT SERVICES.FEL. is also a construction service company with specialization in welding and fabrication, civil engineering works amidst other legitimate concerns. The company is skillfully piloted by a team of seasoned professionals. In conformity with contemporary best practices in the industry, safety is a way of life in FEL and is intergrated into the very depth of our core values as the safe way, is the only way. The company also has and fully implements an intergrated Health, Environment, safety, security and community affairs policy and is living its founder's dreams of providing excellent services.

Management team

The management team are held accountable by the board of directors in order to checkmate our activities. And on a daily basis our management team is generally responsible for putting together our work strategies and ensuring that our objectives are been met.

board of directors

The Board of Directors of Floam Energy Limited is responsible for the organisation of the Company and management of the Company’s operations. The Board is to manage the Company’s affairs in the interests of the Company and all shareholders with the aim of creating long-term shareholder value.
